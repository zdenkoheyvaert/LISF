About EIS Freshwater

Earth Information System (EIS) envisions a cloud-based open source system for enabling integrated Earth system studies and analytics.

EIS Freshwater enables decision-support science related to understanding freshwater variability, extreme events, and the impact of human management on the hydrosphere.
